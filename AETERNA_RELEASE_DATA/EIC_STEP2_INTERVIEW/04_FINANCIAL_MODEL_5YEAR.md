## 8. Key Assumptions

1. **Growth Rate:** 15% MoM in Y1 (declining to 8% MoM by Y5)
2. **Churn:** 1.25% monthly / 15% annual (industry average for SME SaaS)
3. **ARPU:** €119.78 blended monthly, growing 5% annually through mix shift
4. **Gross Margin:** 82% → 91% improving with scale (infrastructure leverage)
5. **CAC:** €180 blended, decreasing 10% annually with brand recognition
6. **Payback:** 1.5 months (significantly below 18-month SaaS benchmark)
7. **Net Revenue Retention:** 115% (existing customers expand usage)
8. **Sales Cycle:** 14 days (SME), 90 days (enterprise)
9. **Infrastructure Cost:** €0.16/user/day, decreasing with scale
10. **Engineering Productivity:** 5:1 feature/engineer ratio vs. traditional SaaS (single codebase advantage)
