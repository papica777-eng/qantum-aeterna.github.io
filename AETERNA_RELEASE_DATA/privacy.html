Privacy Policy & Data Safety

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy governs the suite of applications under the AETERNA Sovereign ecosystem, including but not limited to: AquaLedger, CodeMedic, DeepShield, MindForge, NetGuard, and WealthGuard.

1. Data Collection & Transparency

We believe in absolute data sovereignty. Most of our applications process data exclusively on your device. However, for stability and security, we may collect:

  • Device Identifiers: Unique IDs for crash reporting and security integrity via Google Play Integrity API.
  • Performance Logs: Anonymous data regarding app load times and battery usage.
  • Financial Data (WealthGuard): All financial records are stored locally using encrypted SQLite databases and are never transmitted to our servers.

2. Data Deletion Protocol

Users have the right to delete their data at any time. Since data is stored locally, deleting the application will remove all associated data. For any synced services, you can request manual deletion via delete-my-data@aeterna-sovereign.com.

3. Special Permissions

Some apps (like NetGuard and DeepShield) require advanced permissions:

  • VPN Service: Used locally to filter malicious traffic. No data is intercepted or stored externally.
  • Accessibility Service: Used only for automated security scans if enabled by the user.

4. Data Safety (Google Play Compliance)

In accordance with Google Play's Data Safety requirements, we provide the following declarations:

  • No Data Sharing: We do not share any user data with third-party companies or organizations.
  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted from the applications to our secure Firebase/GCP infrastructure is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols.
  • Data Deletion: Users can request the permanent deletion of their data at any time via the contact method provided in Section 2.
  • Security Audits: Our security-focused apps (NetGuard, DeepShield) undergo regular integrity checks via the Play Integrity API to ensure a compromised environment does not put user data at risk.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

To provide our services, we use Google Cloud Platform (GCP) and Firebase. These providers process data (such as IP addresses and device IDs) solely on our behalf and under strict confidentiality agreements. They are prohibited from using your data for their own marketing purposes.

6. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this policy to reflect changes in our apps or legal requirements. Users will be notified of significant changes through app updates or our official website.
